🔹 WIN Wellington - Te Whare Wai Para Nuku (WCC Sludge Minimisation Facility) Site Visit
📅 Date: 2025-10-08
📍 Location: Freight Drive, Wellington
🏢 Company: Infrastructure New Zealand

📄 Summary:
Join the Wellington Chapter of the Women's Infrastructure Network on Tuesday 7 October from 12:30pm to see the great progress of the WCC Sludge Minimisation Facility since our event in February ’24. The McConnell Dowell/HEB Construction JV with Beca as Design partner on behalf of Wellington City Council are constructing a world-class facility, the first of its type in Aotearoa. Te Whare Wai Para Nuku is located below the existing Moa Point Wastewater Treatment plant. It is designed to reduce the amount of sewage sludge created though the wastewater treatment process, and turn it into a safe, reusable product. This will allow Wellington to reduce its carbon emissions and waste to landfill.
